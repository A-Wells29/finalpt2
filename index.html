The Green Bay Packers are an NFL team based in Green Bay, Wisconsin, playing in the NFC North division. They are one of the league’s oldest and most successful franchises, holding a record 13 league championships, including four Super Bowl victories.


The Packers have played home games at Lambeau Field since 1957, one of the most iconic venues in pro football. The stadium has been expanded several times and now holds over 80,000 fans, famous for cold‑weather games such as the 1967 “Ice Bowl.”
